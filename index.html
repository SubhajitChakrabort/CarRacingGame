🏁 Location Racing Rules

How to Play:

  1. 🎯 Objective: Navigate your green car through traffic while reaching real-world target locations to score points.
  2. 📍 Location Setup: Allow location access to enable GPS-based racing. Target areas are generated around your real location.
  3. 🚗 Controls:
    • • Arrow Keys: Move your car
    • • Up Arrow: Accelerate (0-200 kmph)
    • • Down Arrow: Brake (reduce speed)
    • • Mobile: Use 2x2 touch controls
  4. 🎯 Target System:
    • • Green pulsing areas appear on the road
    • • Drive through them to collect points
    • • Closer targets = higher score multiplier
    • • New targets generate automatically
  5. 📊 Scoring: Base score + distance bonus + speed bonus. Location accuracy affects multiplier.
  6. 💥 Collision: Game ends when hitting red enemy cars. Target progress is saved.
  7. 🆙 Levels: Speed increases every 500 points. More targets appear at higher levels.
  8. 🗺️ Map View: Check your location, targets, and progress on the interactive map.
  9. 🔒 Privacy: Location data is only used for gameplay and never stored or shared.
